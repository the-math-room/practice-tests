# Integrated Math 1 Standards Reference

## Geometry

### Congruence

- **G-CO.1** — Know precise definitions of angle, circle, perpendicular line, parallel line, and line segment.
- **G-CO.2** — Represent transformations in the plane and compare those that preserve distance and angle to those that do not.
- **G-CO.3** — Describe rotations and reflections that carry rectangles, parallelograms, trapezoids, or regular polygons onto themselves.
- **G-CO.4** — Develop definitions of rotations, reflections, and translations.
- **G-CO.5** — Draw transformed figures and specify sequences of transformations.
- **G-CO.6** — Use rigid motions to determine whether figures are congruent.
- **G-CO.7** — Use congruence in terms of rigid motions to justify triangle congruence.
- **G-CO.8** — Explain why ASA, SAS, and SSS work.

### Expressing Geometric Properties with Equations

- **G-GPE.4** — Use coordinates to prove simple geometric theorems algebraically.
- **G-GPE.5** — Prove slope criteria for parallel and perpendicular lines and solve related problems.
- **G-GPE.7** — Compute polygon perimeters and areas of triangles and rectangles using coordinates.

## Statistics and Probability

### Single-variable data

- **S-ID.1** — Represent data with dot plots, histograms, and box plots.
- **S-ID.2** — Compare center and spread of data sets.
- **S-ID.3** — Interpret differences in shape, center, and spread, including outliers.

### Two-variable data

- **S-ID.5** — Use two-way frequency tables and interpret relative frequencies.
- **S-ID.6.a** — Use scatter plots and fit appropriate functions.
- **S-ID.6.b** — Assess fit using residuals.
- **S-ID.6.c** — Fit a linear function when appropriate.

### Linear models

- **S-ID.7** — Interpret slope and intercept in context.
- **S-ID.8** — Compute and interpret correlation coefficient.
- **S-ID.9** — Distinguish correlation from causation.
